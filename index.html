03 — The Four Deployment Phases

From invisible to the
category default

Four phases. Each one builds on the last. By Phase 4, your executive team is the name buyers already trust and it only takes 4hrs/mo of their time to get there.

1
Foundation
MarketFit Spinner™
We build your Market Of One positioning and company narrative so your ideal buyers feel like you're the only logical choice and your competitors become irrelevant.

Most companies sound exactly like their competitors. MarketFit Spinner™ carves out a distinct position — a Market Of One — so when buyers encounter your content, they think: this is exactly who we've been looking for.

  • ICP deep-dive and buyer psychology mapping
  • Competitive landscape analysis to find white space
  • Core narrative and positioning document
  • Messaging hierarchy — what to say, in what order, on which channel
  • Executive bio and personal brand narrative
2
Content Engine
Scroll-Stopping Engine™
We create executive content that builds a realm of influence around your brand so buyers discover you, follow your thinking, and trust your POV.

Content that blends in doesn't compound. We build a creative engine producing high-impact LinkedIn and YouTube content — rooted in your executive's real experience and opinions — that attracts the exact buyers you want to be talking to.

  • Monthly content calendar and ideation based on what buyers actually search
  • Long-form LinkedIn posts ghostwritten in your executive's voice
  • Short-form video scripts and production direction
  • Content repurposing system — one recording, multiple formats
  • 4 hours/month maximum from your executive's calendar
3
Outbound Activation
Warm Outbound System™
We run targeted LinkedIn outbound sequences against high-intent buyers and those who already know your executive from the content. Reply rates move from the industry average of 3–5% to 20–30%.

Cold outbound is broken. Warm outbound flips this. We reach out to prospects who have already seen your content, searching for similar solutions, have a relationship with your executive's brand, and the conversation opens at a completely different temperature.

  • Target account list building against your ICP
  • Buyer intent signals monitored across multiple vendors
  • Personalized and manual LinkedIn outreach, not spray-and-pray
  • Reply management and meeting booking system
  • Monthly pipeline report tied to content performance
4
Paid Amplification
C-suite Paid Ads Strategy™
We retarget your best-performing organic content as paid ads to reach in-market buyers and warm audiences at the moment they're ready to decide, at a fraction of cold CAC.

Organic content builds trust. Paid ads give it velocity. We take what's already working — the posts and videos your audience engaged with most — and put spend behind them. Because you're retargeting warm audiences instead of running cold campaigns, CPMs stay low and conversion rates stay high.

  • Best-performing organic content identified and repurposed as ad creative
  • LinkedIn retargeting audiences — profile visitors, video viewers, content engagers
  • Campaign structure, targeting, and budget allocation
  • A/B testing framework and optimization cadence
  • Revenue attribution tied back to organic content performance
